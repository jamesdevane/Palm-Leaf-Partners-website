Where We Are Building

Phase 1 · Active

National Address Mapping

A target of up to 20 million addresses at a 95% accuracy goal — for the first time in DRC history. Delivered through up to 100 modernized post offices, every one connected by Starlink. Figures indicative; subject to Definitive Agreement.

Explore project →
Phase 2 · Development

National Fiber Backbone

A national fiber backbone connecting six major cities, with microwave reach to 100+ remote sites — and Starlink for last-mile rural coverage across the world's second-largest country.

Explore project →
Phase 2 · Development

Sovereign Data Center

A two-site sovereign cloud — Kinshasa primary, Lubumbashi disaster-recovery — designed to Uptime Institute Tier IV standards, with a 24/7 Security Operations Center. The DRC's data stays in the DRC.

Phase 3 · Planned

Regional Call Centers

Five to ten regional call centers covering every region of the DRC, operating in six languages — built on the address mapping foundation that makes precise emergency dispatch possible.
